Aventure in Treetops :

At Jungle Flight, you’ll experience a breathtaking adventure that takes you over treetops and through canyons. The courses consist of ziplines, canopy walks, and rappels, and will keep your adrenaline pumping throughout the trip. Skyline Jungle Luge Chiang Mai is another thrilling destination where you can combine ziplining with a ride on a toboggan. Plunge through the trees and then race down the mountainside in a gravity-powered cart and experience the landscapes of Chiang Mai in a whole new way. Last but not least, King Kong Smile Zipline brings an element of fun and whimsy to your adventure with its creative designs. The friendly staff will ensure that your ride through the jungle is an unforgettable experience. Of course, safety is paramount in these activities. Strict safety measures are followed on each of these courses so that even the most intense adrenaline junkie can enjoy the experience worry-free. You’ll be equipped with a safety helmet and harness, and trained professionals will be with you every step of the way. Zip-lining in Chiang Mai isn’t only about the thrill, but also about some of the most beautiful views you can imagine. As you glide through the air, you’ll have breathtaking views of Thailand’s mountainous landscape, lush green treetops, hidden waterfalls, and exotic wildlife. You may even be lucky enough to spot a rare bird or two! Zip-lining in Chiang Mai is the perfect way to see Thailand from a new perspective, with a good dose of adrenaline to trunk. So, to all the adventurers out there, don’t miss out on this unique and heart-pounding experience on your next visit to Thailand.


Rock Climbing :

Thailand isn’t only a paradise for those who crave sun-drenched beaches, but also for those who seek adventure amid towering cliffs and breathtaking ocean views. Among the country’s many attractions, rock climbing in Krabi stands out as a prime destination for adrenaline junkies and climbing enthusiasts. Located in the south of the country, Krabi is world famous for its dramatic limestone cliffs that rise majestically from the azure waters of the Andaman Sea. These cliffs offer hundreds of routes with varying degrees of difficulty, suitable for both beginners and experienced climbers. For novice climbers, Railay Beach is the perfect starting point. There are several climbing schools in this area that offer introductory courses and equipment rentals. The 1-2-3 Wall and Phra Nang Beach areas have routes that are ideal for beginners and offer an exciting and safe introduction to the sport. Experienced climbers, on the other hand, can try their hand at Tonsai Wall or Thai Wall. These places have overhanging rocks and complicated routes that will test even the most experienced climbers. The breathtaking view from the top of the emerald sea stretching to the horizon is well worth the effort of the climb. When it comes to safety, the climbing schools in Krabi take it very seriously. They provide all the necessary equipment, including helmets, harnesses and climbing shoes. Before you start climbing, you’ll receive a safety briefing and learn how to use the equipment properly. It’s also recommended to climb with a partner or under the supervision of a trained instructor, especially if you’re just starting out. Climbing the limestone cliffs of Krabi is more than just an adrenaline-pumping adventure; it’s an opportunity to immerse yourself in Thailand’s natural beauty and experience a side of the country that goes beyond its bustling cities and quiet beaches. Whether you’re an experienced climber or a curious beginner, get ready for an unforgettable experience: rock climbing in Krabi


Paddling Through Paradise :

If you want to discover the natural wonders of southern Thailand from a unique perspective, sea canoeing in Phang Nga Bay promises an unforgettable adventure. In this eco-friendly activity, you’ll glide across the azure waters of the Andaman Sea and explore a labyrinth of limestone cliffs, hidden caves and tranquil lagoons. Whether you’re based in Phuket, Krabi or Koh Samui, several companies offer kayaking adventures that range from a few hours to a full day. The calm waters of Phang Nga Bay are an ideal and safe environment for paddling even for those who have no experience in canoeing. Guided by knowledgeable staff, you’ll navigate through sea caves leading to hidden lagoons that are home to a variety of flora and fauna. Guides will ensure that you not only experience the breathtaking beauty of the bay, but also understand its ecological importance by educating visitors about the unique marine and bird life in the area. Paddling under towering limestone cliffs and through mysterious sea caves, you’ll experience up close the incredible karst formations that have been sculpted by nature over thousands of years. Canoeing in Phang Nga Bay is an adventure that combines physical activity, breathtaking natural beauty and immersion in the local ecology. So grab a paddle, don a life jacket and get ready to navigate the calm, emerald waters of this unique seascape and experience southern Thailand like never before.

Off Roading Through Thaliland's North Jewal :

If you’re an adrenaline junkie looking for an exhilarating off-road experience, ATV (All-Terrain Vehicle) adventures in Chiang Mai are for you. These tours offer an exciting way to explore the region’s diverse landscape, combining the thrill of a high-speed ride with the natural beauty of northern Thailand. ATV adventures in Chiang Mai are suitable for all experience levels, from beginners to off-road veterans. You can choose from a variety of routes, each offering a unique perspective on the region’s breathtaking landscapes. Whether you’re navigating through dense jungle terrain, crossing rivers, or climbing mountain trails, each trip guarantees a heart-pounding experience.
